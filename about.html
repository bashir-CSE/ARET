About ARET

Who We Are

Automation Research & Embedded Technologies (ARET) is a leading educational institution specializing in skill development and innovative research in the fields of industrial automation and embedded systems. We are committed to empowering the present generation with cutting-edge technical skills through online and offline education.

With experienced instructors, hands-on projects, and state-of-the-art equipment, we provide industry-based technical training to help students master industrial automation and embedded systems.

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To provide high-quality, industry-relevant technical education in automation and embedded systems, empowering individuals with the skills needed to excel in their careers and contribute to technological advancement.
