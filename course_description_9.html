Electrical Engineering Applications In Textile Machines

Course Overview

Master electrical engineering applications in textile machinery

30 Days 10 Seats BDT 4000.00

Course Description

"Electrical engineers working in textile factories must have a strong understanding of textile machines.
Electrical diagrams are crucial for understanding the complex systems used in these machines. In this course, you will learn about wiring diagrams, ladder diagrams, schematic diagrams, and control systems specific to textile machinery. The course combines theory with practical training to help engineers and technicians install, troubleshoot, and maintain textile machines."

Prerequisites:

Basic understanding of electrical systems, automation, and control systems.
